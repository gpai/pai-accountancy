What We Offer

Audit

Since 1980, performing audits has been the cornerstone of our practice. We’re trained to exam findings and identify opportunities for improvements. Every audit complies with professional standards, and is geared toward uncovering your business’s strengths in order to achieve maximum efficiency.

Tax

Our tax specialist have the breadth and depth of federal tax, state and local tax, as well as international taxation, and compensation and benefits expertise to meet your compliance and specialty tax service needs. To us, “tax time” is all the time.

Accounting/Bookeeping

Accurate record-keeping is essential to a successful business yet can also be complicated and time-consuming. Pai Accountancy LLP can help you with the organization and day-to-day tasks of bookkeeping so that you can focus on your core business. We’re dedicated to assisting our clients in improving their financial reporting and offering personal quality service.

Consulting

Our consulting professionals provide advice on everything from strategic issues to choosing and implementing the best technology system for your business. Our consulting services include: employee benefits, executive compensation, systems reviews, Sarbanes-Oxley compliance and internal control studies. We focus on helping high-tech start-up companies obtain funding, plan budget forecasts, and help merge acquisitions and IPO. We offer sound judgment and astute industry insight.

Payroll Services

Pai Accountancy LLP’s payroll services can help you reduce the time spent on administration through developing and implementing a computerized payroll system that will facilitate processing, timely payment, and preparation of tax returns.

Estate and Trust Planning and Tax Preparation

Effective estate and trust planning can ensure financial security for your loved ones. For businesses, it can maintain a smooth succession of ownership. Pai Accountancy LLP’s role is to help you navigate the complex and shifting tax laws to facilitate the transfer of assets and minimize the tax liability of your beneficiaries.
